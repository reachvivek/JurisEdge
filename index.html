Frequently Asked Questions

Common legal questions and expert answers

How can I protect myself from cyber crimes?

Use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, avoid suspicious links, and report any cyber incidents to the cybercrime portal immediately.

Can I file for divorce without a lawyer?

While possible, legal representation is highly recommended for divorce proceedings to ensure proper documentation and protection of your rights.

Do I need a personal lawyer for my business?

For regular business operations, having a legal advisor on retainer helps prevent legal issues and ensures compliance with regulations.

What are the typical legal consultation fees?

Consultation fees vary by case complexity. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden charges. Contact us for a detailed fee structure.

Is there a cap on legal fees for cases?

Fee structures depend on case type and complexity. We provide detailed fee agreements upfront with clear billing practices.

Are you licensed to practice in Jharkhand?

Yes, we are licensed to practice in Jharkhand High Court and all subordinate courts within the state jurisdiction.
